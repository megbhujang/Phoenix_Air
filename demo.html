ABOUT US


Phoenix Air (PA) is a major Indian airline headquartered in New Delhi, India, within the Dallas Fort Worth metroplex. It is the world's largest airline when measured by fleet size, revenue, scheduled passengers carried, scheduled passenger-kilometers flown, and number of destinations served. Phoenix, together with its regional partners, operates an extensive international and domestic network with almost 6,800 flights per day to nearly 350 destinations in more than 50 countries. Phoenix Airlines is a founding member of Oneworld alliance, the third largest airline alliance in the world. Regional service is operated by independent and subsidiary carriers under the brand name Eagle.

About the tickets

"Tickets" are now replaced by boarding passes, generally issued to you 24 hours in advance of the flight.

You can print them off at home, or retain them on your phone, or print them off at the airport, either at a kiosk or at the "ticket" counter. Information Desk is available near Row 'C' and Information Desk at Airport Village. The purpose of the Help Desk is to assist travelers with a variety of needs and concerns pertaining to Phoenix Airport.
